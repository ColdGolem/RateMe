Privacy Policy

SaaS (RateMe) is an entertainment application designed for fun photo rating and decision making.

Images uploaded by users are processed locally in the application and are not stored on any server.

The app does not collect, store, or share personal data from users.

If advertising services such as Google AdMob are enabled in the future, those services may collect anonymous usage information according to their own privacy policies.

This app is intended for entertainment purposes only. Ratings and messages are randomly generated and do not represent real analysis.
